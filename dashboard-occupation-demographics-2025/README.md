# Parent Occupation Demographics Dashboard — School Network (2025)

## Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing the **occupational profile of 5,469 parents** across a 5-brand, 15-campus private school network in Panama.

Built using anonymized operational data to provide executive teams with a clear picture of the socioeconomic profile of enrolled families by brand and campus.

## Dashboard Preview
![Dashboard Preview](dashboard-preview.png)

## Key Insights
- **Merchant/Trader** is the most common parent occupation (661 records — 12.1% of total)
- **Self-Employed** ranks second with 490 records, reflecting a strong entrepreneurial profile
- **Greenfield Academy** dominates with 64.8% of total parent records (3,544)
- **Lawyers, Physicians and Managers** are concentrated in Greenfield - Edison Park and Greenfield - Santiago campuses
- **Homemakers** represent 7.2% of the network — relevant for understanding family availability for school engagement

## Visualizations
- KPI card: Total occupation records
- Horizontal bar chart: Top 10 occupations across the network
- Treemap: Distribution by brand and campus
- Donut chart: % distribution by brand
- Detail table: Occupation breakdown by campus with counts
- Slicers: Filter by occupation, brand, and campus

## Data
- **Source:** Anonymized CRM export (Salesforce)
- **Records:** 5,469 parent occupation entries
- **Fields:** Brand, Campus, Occupation
- **Year:** 2025
- **Note:** Occupations translated from Spanish to English for international portfolio use
